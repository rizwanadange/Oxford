About Oxford Coaching

Pioneers in Navodaya & Sainik School coaching with 15+ years of excellence

Join Our Legacy

Our Journey

Founded in 2010, Oxford Coaching Classes began with a vision to provide quality coaching for competitive entrance exams to students from rural and semi-urban backgrounds. What started as a small coaching center has now transformed into a premier institution with a proven track record of success.

  • 15+ years of excellence in competitive coaching
  • 3000+ students trained successfully
  • 500+ selections in prestigious schools
  • State-of-the-art residential facility
  • Expert faculty with 10+ years experience

Our Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive our institution

Our Vision

To become the most trusted coaching institute for residential school entrance exams, nurturing disciplined future leaders who contribute positively to society and the nation.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, value-based education that prepares students academically, physically, and emotionally for success in competitive exams and life.

Our Promise

Quality education with personalized attention, modern facilities, and a disciplined environment that transforms ordinary students into extraordinary achievers.
