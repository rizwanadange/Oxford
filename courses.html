Exam Details

Understanding the entrance examination patterns

JNVST Pattern

  • Mental Ability Test
  • Arithmetic Test
  • Language Test
  • No negative marking
  • Class 6: 80 Questions
  • Duration: 2 hours

AISSEE Pattern

  • Mathematics: 50 marks
  • English: 25 marks
  • Intelligence: 25 marks
  • General Knowledge: 25 marks
  • Total: 125 marks
  • Physical & Medical Tests

Physical Standards

  • Running (1.6 km)
  • Push-ups & Sit-ups
  • Medical Examination
  • Height & Weight norms
  • Eyesight requirements
  • Overall fitness
